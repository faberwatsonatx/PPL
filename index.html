🏁 Traffic Pattern & Right-of-Way

Pattern: Upwind → Crosswind → Downwind → Base → Final (left turns unless noted)
Enter: 45° to midpoint of downwind at 1,000 AGL
Right-of-way: Balloon > Glider > Airship > Airplane (less maneuverable wins)
Head-on: both go RIGHT · Overtaking: pass on RIGHT
Light guns: Steady green = CLEARED · Flashing green = return/taxi · Steady red = STOP/give way · Flashing red = DANGER
Pilot-controlled lighting: 7 clicks HIGH · 5 clicks MED · 3 clicks LOW (on CTAF)
